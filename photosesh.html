Photo Session Overview

Are you looking to capture some of life's little moments? Whether you want to capture your favourite place, take family or pet photos, or even just memorialize a walk down your favourite beach, Blue Rose Photography is here to make sure your memories last a lifetime!

Equipment includes a Canon Rebel TlOO DSLR camera with two lens options (Canon EFS 18-SSmm, or Canon 75-300mm Zoom Lens), as well as a 58" Insignia tripod. At this time, we do not provide lighting, but are willing to work with you to create the atmosphere you desire!*

Further down you will find a list of our most popular packages, as well as pricing. Please keep in mind that these prices are baselines, and that each shoot has its own challenges and requirements which may alter pricing. We strongly suggest contacting us beforehand for a consult.

*Complex photo editing may be subject to additional fees. If you are interested, please use the contact form at the bottom of the page so that we can discuss what you're looking for.
